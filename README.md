# Inventory Command Center 📦

Inventory Command Center is an AI-powered inventory analytics and replenishment dashboard designed for Quick Commerce and retail operations. It enables operations teams to monitor inventory health across multiple warehouses, identify stockout risks, and make data-driven replenishment decisions through an intuitive, real-time dashboard.

The platform analyzes SKU-level inventory data, lead times, incoming stock, and warehouse performance to proactively recommend replenishment actions before stockouts occur. By transforming raw inventory data into actionable insights, it helps reduce lost sales, optimize inventory levels, and improve supply chain efficiency.

---

## ✨ Features

* 📊 Real-time operations dashboard with key inventory KPIs
* ⚠️ AI-driven stockout risk detection and prioritization
* 📦 Automated replenishment quantity recommendations
* 🏢 Multi-city and multi-warehouse inventory monitoring
* 📈 Interactive analytics for inventory health and SKU performance
* 🚨 Critical alert system for high-risk inventory
* 🔍 Search and filter inventory by SKU, warehouse, city, and risk level
* 📉 Lead time vs. inventory coverage analysis
* 🌍 City-wise inventory health visualization
* 📋 Actionable recommendations with "Place Order" workflows

---

## 🚀 Why Inventory Command Center?

Managing inventory across multiple warehouses is challenging, especially in Quick Commerce where stock availability directly impacts customer satisfaction. Traditional inventory management often relies on manual analysis, making it difficult to identify stockout risks before they occur.

Inventory Command Center provides a centralized view of inventory operations by continuously analyzing stock levels, lead times, incoming inventory, and warehouse performance. Operations teams can proactively replenish inventory, minimize stockouts, reduce excess inventory, and improve fulfillment efficiency.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Monitor inventory health across cities and warehouses
* Identify high-risk SKUs before stockouts occur
* Track replenishment quantities required for each warehouse
* Compare lead times with inventory coverage
* Analyze stock distribution by city and warehouse
* Prioritize urgent replenishment actions using AI-generated recommendations
